About

DiversiTR is a Shiny app for visualizing the expression and diversity of T-cell receptors from Rep-Seq data.

How to

You first need to upload the Data, there are two ways to do it :

  • in the “Upload RDS file” tab, the app expects a .RDS file containg a RepSeqExperiment object,
  • in the “Upload text file(s)” tab, the app expects .tsv or .txt files containing Rep-Seq data from ClonotypeR or MiXCR, before uploading you will need to specify the origin of the data, the chain (alpha or beta) and provide a file containing informations about the samples. After a successful upload a RepSeqExperiment object is built, you then have the option to convert and download it as a .RDS file by clicking “Download RDS”.
